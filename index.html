How Much Does a Beauty Salon Website Cost in the UK? (2025 Honest Breakdown)

Published by Aura Systems Design  ·  8 min read

If you've started googling "how much does a salon website cost" and found yourself more confused than when you started — you're not alone.

The truth is, the price range is enormous. You can spend £249 or £5,000 for what appears, on the surface, to be the same thing. This article gives you the honest breakdown — no jargon, no upselling — so you know exactly what you're paying for at every price point.

The four ways to get a salon website in the UK

1. DIY website builders (£7–£40/month)

Platforms like Wix and Squarespace let you build your own website using drag-and-drop templates.

What you get: A basic website you control yourself. Looks reasonable if you put the time in.

The hidden cost: Your time. Most salon owners spend 15–30 hours building it, then let it go stale.

Best for: Mobile beauty therapists or sole traders just starting out who have more time than money.

2. Freelance web designers (£500–£2,500 one-off)

A freelancer will build a custom-designed website to your brief, usually delivered in 2–6 weeks.

What you get: A custom design built around your brand.

What you don't get: Ongoing support. Most freelancers hand over the finished site and move on.

Best for: Salons with a one-off budget who are comfortable managing the site themselves afterwards.

3. Salon-specialist web agencies (£35–£120/month or £3,200–£4,200 one-off)

Agencies that work specifically with hair and beauty businesses.

What you get: A website built by people who understand the beauty industry.

What you don't get: A booking system that's truly integrated with your brand.

Best for: Established salons who want a professional presence and can commit to a monthly fee.

4. A complete beauty business platform (£499–£2,999 setup + £49–£399/month)

This is different from a website. It's your entire digital business — custom website, integrated booking, deposit collection, SMS reminders, a reception card terminal, client management, staff scheduling, social media management and a dashboard that shows you exactly where your money is going. All under your own brand. No commission.

Best for: Salon owners who are serious about their business and want a system that grows with them.

Do I need any technical knowledge? +
None at all. We handle everything — design, build, hosting, and setup. You just tell us about your business and we do the rest. We also train you on how to use the booking system.
How long does it take to go live? +
Most websites are live within 3–5 working days. Full Suite allows 5–7 days. For Aura Complete with full business setup, allow 7–10 days. We keep you updated throughout.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Yes, absolutely. You can start with Presence and upgrade to any higher package whenever you're ready. We'll apply a credit for the setup fee difference.

We create, design and schedule 12 posts per month across your Instagram and Facebook accounts. Content is tailored to your brand, services and promotions. Paid advertising spend is separate and billed directly to your ad account — we manage the campaigns, you set the budget.

Stripe charges 1.5% + 20p per UK card transaction online, and 1.4% + 10p for in-person payments via the included WisePOS E terminal. These fees go directly to Stripe — Aura Systems Design never takes a percentage of your bookings. Ever.

No long-term contracts on monthly rolling plans. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. The 12 month plan offers ~20% savings with a minimum term commitment.

All plans include email and WhatsApp support. Full Suite and Aura Complete clients get priority support, a dedicated account manager and regular strategy calls. We're here when you need us.
