Questions

Frequently
asked

Do I need any technical knowledge? +
None at all. We handle everything — design, build, hosting, and setup. You just tell us about your business and we do the rest. We also train you on how to use the booking system.
How long does it take to go live? +
Most websites are live within 3–5 working days. For the Full Suite package with CRM and payments, allow 5–7 days. We keep you updated throughout the entire process.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Yes, absolutely. You can start with Presence and upgrade to Bookable or Full Suite whenever you're ready. We'll apply a credit for the setup fee difference.
What payment provider do you use? +
We integrate Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment processors. Stripe handles deposits and full payments securely, and funds transfer directly to your bank account within 2 business days.
Is the monthly fee on a contract? +
No long-term contracts. Our monthly plans are rolling and you can cancel any time. We're confident you'll love the results — but we never lock you in.
What support do you offer? +
All plans include email support. Full Suite clients get priority support and a dedicated onboarding call. We're here when you need us.
